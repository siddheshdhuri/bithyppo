GLOBAL REGULATIONS SHAPING MARKETING

Marketing in a regulated world

Marketing has evolved into personalized marketing where every individual is now a segment. However, handling personal data requires protection from data theft and compliance with data privacy laws.

As the world tackles challenges of personal data protection, the global data protection laws are making consumer’s data increasingly difficult to obtain, while the fines for compliance infraction are getting steeper.

With the EU GDPR (Global Data Protection Regulations) coming into effect, organizations find it difficult to directly market without explicit consent. Organizations see a shift towards driving inbound marketing using channels such as social media. On the other hand, navigating social media is very challenging, given the scale and complexity of data. For this reason, organizations will need to use social media marketing more effectively and intelligently.

Applying artifical intelligence in marketing can help you evolve in this new regulated space of marketing.
